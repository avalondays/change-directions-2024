Lucas Baker

eliolynx.com

Lucas Baker (artist name: Elio Lynx) is a Graphic Designer & Multimedia Artist, who has a rich history in performance art and photography, making their work a unique fusion of kinesthetic and photographic elements. Lynx loves to layout design for both print and social media, as well as photo edit in ways that alter perceptions. Their design style can be described as a blend of punk, provocative, brutalist, rooted in Swiss design principles. In their personal explorations, Lynx creates work that embodies the intricacies of queer representation & sexuality.
