Demitrius Paris

dparis.co

Demitrius Paris is something. Definitely something. He’s a 22 year old “artist/designer” (he doesn’t consider himself those things) that doesn’t know what’s going on most of the time. Chaos seems to be his natural habitat, keeping everyone on their toes. Even himself. He does like making people laugh, sometimes inadvertently. Demitrius’ thought process (as all over as it may be) allows for his work to be spontaneous. Which can be and has been to a fault at its extreme. He does have a tendency to go on tangents though, talking about nothing for hours. A talent and a curse.

Loss of Biodiversity

Environment

The environment has been at the forefront of today’s issues to tackle. Just saying “helping the environment” doesn’t speak to anything. It’s just a broad term masking an area of vagueness. In that, we have the loss of biodiversity. The loss of biodiversity refers to the decline in the variety and abundance of living organisms and ecosystems on Earth. Biodiversity, short for biological diversity, encompasses the variety of life at various levels, including genetic diversity within species, the diversity of species within ecosystems, and the diversity of ecosystems at a global scale.
