Yixiao Wang

ywnnydesigner.com

Yixiao Wang is a visual and creative designer from a coastal city in China, who studied Visual Communication Design at Arizona State University, USA. During a project at Xiaomi in 2023, she discovered her passion for UI/UX design, shaping her career path. Her experience in user experience and interface design has become a cornerstone of her professional growth. In her personal life, Yixiao is an avid photographer, capturing the beauty of the seaside and life's romantic moments. Her photography is a reflection of her unique perspective and love for aesthetics, which she skillfully integrates into her designs. Yixiao believes in creating functional and visually appealing works that convey emotions and stories, embodying the power of great design.

Ocean Pollution

Environmental

Ocean pollution has become a severe social and environmental issue globally. Caused by factors such as industrial wastewater discharge, accumulation of plastic waste, and oil spills from ships, it poses a significant threat to the health of marine ecosystems and global biodiversity. The impact of ocean pollution extends to the contamination of food chains, disruption of ecological balance, and even direct threats to human health.


Addressing this issue requires global cooperation and comprehensive strategies, including strengthening legal regulations, promoting sustainable use of marine resources, and enhancing public education and awareness. This challenge is not only a test of environmental conservation efforts but also a measure of human innovation and social responsibility.
