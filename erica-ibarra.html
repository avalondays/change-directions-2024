Erica Ibarra

Instagram

Erica Ibarra is a freelance graphic designer based in Charlotte, North Carolina. This spring, she's wrapping up her Bachelor of Science in Design at Arizona State University, where she's honed her skills in bringing ideas to life through visuals. When she's not bringing ideas to life on the digital canvas, you can catch her indulging in the finer things in life - from brunching, or exploring new destinations and soaking up the sun on vacation to hunting for treasures at thrift stores. Living life with a positive mindset is her mantra, and she strives to infuse every project with that same energy and enthusiasm.

Effects of Child Abuse on Adulthood

Human Rights, Mental Health

Choosing to explore the effects of child abuse on adulthood underscores the profound significance of this issue, highlighting its pervasive and far-reaching consequences. By promoting awareness and understanding, advocating for policy changes, and providing support for survivors, my project aims to contribute to a more compassionate and informed approach to addressing child abuse in society, fostering healing and empowerment.
