Eunice has harbored a passion for the creative arts since her early years. Exposed to animation and digital media during her upbringing, entering the creative field became the ideal scenario in her mind when contemplating various career paths. Psyche Pop-Up

NASA Psyche Mission

The Psyche mission embarks on a journey to explore a metal-rich asteroid, situated in orbit between Mars and Jupiter. Referred to as asteroid 16 Psyche, the world is composed of nickel and iron; This metallic composition has scientists to indicate that it was once a former core of an ancient planet. The Psyche mission comes in view to provide the rare opportunity to gain understanding of the history and creation of Earth and their neighbors. To share interest in this metallic world, visual elements provided in the form of crafted Pop-Up art will be used to promote and spread knowledge of the mission in a fun and interesting way.
