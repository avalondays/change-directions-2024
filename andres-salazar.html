Andres Salazar

andressalazar.world

Andres Salazar is a graphic designer and visual artist from Dallas, Texas. Through his studies and work for ASU, he developed a love for print and packaging design, and plans to further pursue these disciplines in his career. When not designing, he enjoys finding inspiration from media and the world around him, which he channels into personal art. For the future, he works to sharpen his skills in design and art in order to develop projects and products that perfectly encapsulate himself, his ideas, and his identity.
