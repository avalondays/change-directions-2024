Forced Labor and Organ Trafficking in Myanmar

Human Rights

The allure of high salaries has tragically misled many young people in China, drawing them into a perilous trap in Northern Myanmar. This issue is not singular in its cause but is a complex web of deceit and exploitation. Upon arrival, these young individuals face a grim reality far removed from the promises made to them. They are subjected to forced labor, a gross violation of human rights, stripping them of their freedom and dignity.


But the exploitation does not end there. In a horrifying escalation of abuse, those who are no longer able to work due to exhaustion or ill health are then subjected to the most heinous of acts - the illegal trade of their organs. This brutal practice preys on the vulnerable, treating them not as human beings but as mere commodities.
