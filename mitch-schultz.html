Mitch Schultz

Portfolio

Mitch Schultz, originally from Phoenix, Arizona, now calls Seattle, Washington, home. Music and graphic design have been his passions since he can remember, inspired greatly by icons like David Gilmour of Pink Floyd and George Harrison of The Beatles. Living in two vastly different landscapes has enriched his creativity, constantly offering new perspectives to incorporate into his work. Whether he's composing melodies or crafting designs, he draws from the world around him to fuel his artistic expression.
