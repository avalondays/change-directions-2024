Tyler Wagner

tylerwagner.me

Tyler Wagner is a visual and experience designer developed out of the sunny city of Phoenix. He studied the science of Visual Communication Design through ASU where he became grounded in all aspects of design. During the summer of 2023, Tyler traveled to the beautiful city of Charleston, where he indulged in his interest in UX/UI design and application development. This experience has become the foundation for his real world design journey as he aims to create exciting and lasting user experiences for real world products. Outside of design, Tyler fills his days with golf, photography, his car, and his Golden Retriever, Harlee.
