Cheyenne Farnsworth

designsbycheyenne.com

Cheyenne Farnsworth is from Glendale, Arizona. She graduated from Deer Valley High School in 2020 along with graduating from Rio Salado Community College with her Associates degree. Her passion for design ignited during her freshman year of high school when she enrolled in a graphic design elective course, sparking a deep love for the field. This passion led her to pursue a Bachelor's degree in design at Arizona State University, with a graduation date in May of 2024. Looking ahead, she aims to fulfill her aspiration of becoming a graphic design instructor, pursuing further education with a Master's degree starting in Fall 2024.

HIV/AIDS Awareness

Social, Education

HIV/AIDS awareness campaigns educate individuals and communities about the virus and syndrome, covering prevention, transmission, testing, treatment, and support services. Crucial due to its global impact, HIV/AIDS awareness combats stigma, promotes testing, encourages safe sex practices, and facilitates access to treatment. By empowering individuals with knowledge, it enables informed decisions, supports those living with HIV/AIDS, and advocates for research and funding. Promoting community engagement and solidarity, HIV/AIDS awareness fosters a collective effort to combat the epidemic, aiming to save lives, reduce transmission rates, and create inclusive environments for all affected individuals.
