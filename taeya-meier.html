Taeya Meier

taeyameier.com

Born and raised in Minnesota, Taeya Meier has always loved the arts. From being a competitive dancer for 15 years, to drawing in her spare time, she decided to move across the country to pursue a degree in Graphic Design here at Arizona State University. She hopes to transform her love for creativity into a career, dedicated to the art of design.

Gun Violence

Social, Political

This exploration dives into the dynamic relationship between gun violence and the political landscape in the United States. Tracing the historical evolution of gun-related incidents, the analysis centers on the intricate interplay of legislative developments, interest group influence, and state level policies. By scrutinizing the connections between politics and gun violence, the research seeks to uncover patterns, trends, and pivotal moments in the on-going discourse. This investigation aims to deepen our understanding of the complex factors influencing gun violence, contributing to informed discussions and the pursuit of effective solutions in the realm of public safety and firearm regulation.
