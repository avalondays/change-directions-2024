Luke Padwe

padwe.design

Luke is an imaginative and dynamic graphic designer, known for ingenious concepts and unique approach to visuals. Pursuing his education at the prestigious Herberger Institute for Design and the Arts at Arizona State University, studying Graphic Design and Visual Communication. He has developed designs for large-scale events, marketing/promotional material, print material, video/animation, branding/visual identity, and much more. Armed with a profound grasp of design principles and an unyielding passion for creativity, he creates designs that are both recognizable and memorable.

Artificial Intelligence

Technology

In today's world, AI permeates our lives in ways we may not even realize. With an estimated 15 billion devices operating worldwide, and around 4.2 billion of these equipped with AI-powered assistance, the integration of AI is both remarkable and disconcerting. The public's perception of AI platform usage at around 33% starkly contrasts with the reality of 77%, highlighting the rapid and sometimes unnoticed infiltration of AI into our daily routines.


This swift integration raises concerns about the prevalence of bias in AI-generated content, with 63% of individuals expressing worry about potential inaccuracies. As we delve deeper into the realm of AI, it's evident that we're facing an existential challenge that could redefine humanity's future, making the urgency to address AI bias more critical than ever
