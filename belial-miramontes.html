Belial Miramontes

belialm.com

A designer from west Texas, Belial is always exploring sources of creativity and interest. He is drawn to various aesthetics and appreciates distinctive styles and expression. His interest for design works roots from his upbringing and exposure to metal music and digital media. Following his move to Arizona, Belial studied Visual Communication Design at Arizona State University. Outside of design, you can find him sewing, exploring SoundCloud, or at a show.

Art Censorship

Social, Human Rights

Artist Censorship impacts the vitality and diversity of a society’s cultural landscape. When creative expressions are subjected to censorship, it suppresses the voices and perspectives that contribute to the dynamic culture and evolution of the arts. These censorships hinder the community’s ability to freely express themselves, and restrict the public’s access to a wide array of creative and historical content. With censorship of past content and still occurring in the present, this can influence broader censorship regulation and limit creatives in the future
