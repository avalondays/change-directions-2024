The Avoidance of Therapy

Mental Health

The perception of mental illness and therapy has been quite negative for the general public for as long as it has been around. Many of those who need therapy feel the need to avoid it at all costs. Whether that be because of stigma, financial responsibility, the lack of resources or fear, those who need the most help are not receiving it. The only way to solve the problem and to make those who need help feel safe and comfortable is to change the way people think about mental illness and to provide more support and resources not only to the mentally ill, but to mental health providers.
