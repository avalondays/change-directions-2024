Tya Rowitz

tyarowitz.com

Tya Rowitz is a creative and multifaceted designer from Phoenix, Arizona with a passion for art direction and digital marketing. Her journey in graphic design began at Arizona State University, where she developed a strong foundation in both traditional and digital design techniques. With experience in various industries, she excels in creating engaging brand identities, social media content, and innovative digital campaigns. Outside of her professional life, Tya loves to travel, and considers herself a bit of a “foodie”, seeking inspiration from the diverse cultures, cuisines, and landscapes she encounters.
