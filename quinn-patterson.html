Quinn Patterson

quinnpatterson.com

Quinn is a graphic designer from Tucson, Arizona who has studied visual communication design at Arizona State University. She has a background in visual arts which continues to be her main hobby. She has worked in-house as an intern for ASSA ABLOY Opening Solutions where she further discovered her love for designing physical print media. She is interested in design as a tool to communicate important issues and express yourself and your connection with the world, and hopes to seek out work in printing.
