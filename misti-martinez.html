Misti Martinez

Misti Martinez is an aspiring designer originally from rural Idaho, now based out of Savannah, Georgia. She will graduate with her BSD in Graphic Design in the spring of 2024. She fell in love with art and painting as a child and has continued to learn and grow her skillset through various personal projects, as well as stepping up to design logos and fliers for teams within her workgroup at Intuit. When she is not working or designing, Misti loves to play video games, travel, explore, and spend time outdoors with her family and friends.
