The Decline of Academic Freedom

Education, Discrimination

Academic freedom is the freedom of students to pursue knowledge wherever it may lead, without fear of retaliation or discipline. It is the very cornerstone of scholarly inquiry, research, and innovation. As the decline for Academic freedom continues, students lose access to foster critical thinking, empathy, and a diverse understanding of the world, hindering their abilities to develop essential skills such as critical analysis and empathy. Moreover, the quality of education suffers, and societal development continues to stagnate. We must protect this freedom in order to ensure that academia remains a space where perspectives and ideas can truly flourish.
