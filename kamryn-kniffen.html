Transgender History

Social, Human Rights

In recent years, internet personalities and news outlets alike have spread misinformation about transgender individuals in an attempt to cause fear and confusion. A common claim is that transgender identity is a new fad primarily of Western nations, and that it is a movement targeting youth and attempting to induct as many people as it can.


The reality is that transgender identities have existed around the world for centuries. Many cultures, especially non-western, have defined roles and terms for both binary and nonbinary individuals, and the medical world of hormone therapy and surgery goes back much further than many know.
