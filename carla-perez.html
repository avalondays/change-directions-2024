Carla Perez

carlaprz.com

Carla is a 21-year-old student, born in Arizona. She comes from a Hispanic/Latino family and is a first-generation college student. Since childhood, Carla harbored a profound love for drawing, despite her uncertainty, Carla pursued a higher education and eventually found passion for graphic design. Her journey led her to study at Arizona State University, where she's about to obtain her Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Design. Her creative spirit fuels a desire to bring her visions to life, consistently seeking innovative approaches to design and storytelling. With a boundless imagination and a keen eye for detail. Carla continues to explore the endless possibilities within the realm of graphic design.

Child Trafficking

Human Rights

Child trafficking is a heinous crime that involves the recruitment, transportation, transfer, harboring, or receipt of children for the purpose of exploitation. It deprives children of their basic rights, subjecting them to various forms of abuse. Child trafficking occurs globally, often targeting specific groups of people.


Understanding the dynamics of child trafficking is crucial in combating this violation of human rights and protecting the most vulnerable members of our society. Ensuring the safety of children is critical in the fight against child trafficking. Safety focuses into the different safety measures and interventions aimed at preventing children from becoming victims of trafficking schemes while also assisting those who are at risk or have already been trafficked.
